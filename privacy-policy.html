BLVDE Automation Tool - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

Privacy First: BLVDE Automation Tool is a local application. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any external servers.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

The Tool does NOT collect or store:

  • Personal identification information
  • TikTok passwords or credentials
  • Your email address or contact details
  • Analytics or usage tracking data
  • Location data
  • Device information

2. Local Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your computer, including:

  • Configuration: API keys and settings in backend/config.yaml
  • Post Queue: Scheduled posts in local SQLite database
  • Videos: Generated videos saved in output/ folder
  • Exports: Excel files saved to your chosen location

You have full control over this data and can delete it at any time.

3. TikTok OAuth Authentication

When you connect your TikTok account:

  • Authentication happens through TikTok's official OAuth 2.0 flow
  • Your TikTok password is NEVER shared with the Tool
  • We receive only an access token to perform authorized actions
  • The access token is stored locally in encrypted format
  • You can revoke access anytime in your TikTok settings

4. Data We Access from TikTok

With your permission, the Tool accesses:

  • Basic Profile: Username and profile picture (for display purposes)
  • Video Upload: Ability to upload videos to your account
  • Video Publish: Ability to publish videos on your behalf

This data is used solely to provide the Tool's functionality and is not shared with anyone.

5. Third-Party Services

The Tool interacts with:

  • TikTok API: For video posting and account access
  • TikTok Shop: For product research (read-only)

These services have their own privacy policies. Please review them:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy: https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy

6. Data Security

We implement security best practices:

  • API credentials stored in local configuration files
  • No data transmission to external servers
  • OAuth tokens encrypted and stored securely
  • Regular security updates and patches

7. Children's Privacy

The Tool is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

8. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All data is stored locally on your device
  • Delete: Remove the Tool folder to delete all local data
  • Revoke: Disconnect TikTok access through your TikTok account settings
  • Export: All data is in standard formats (YAML, SQLite, MP4)

9. Analytics and Tracking

The Tool does NOT use:

  • Google Analytics or similar tracking
  • Cookies (except session cookies for local authentication)
  • Third-party advertising or tracking scripts
  • Usage telemetry or error reporting

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the Tool or legal requirements. The "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly.

11. Open Source and Transparency

The Tool's code is available for review. You can inspect exactly what data is accessed and how it's used.

12. Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, contact the tool administrator.

Summary: Your data stays on your computer. We don't collect, track, or share anything. You're in complete control.
